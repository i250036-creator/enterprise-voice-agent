HORIZON HEALTH CLINIC — BILLING FAQ

Payment Methods Accepted:
- Cash
- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard)
- Bank transfer
- Insurance direct billing (for accepted providers)

Billing Cycle:
For patients on payment plans (used for larger procedures like surgeries or extended treatment), invoices are generated monthly and due within 15 days of issue. Late payments beyond 30 days incur a 2 percent monthly late fee.

Refund Policy:
- If an appointment is cancelled more than 24 hours in advance, any pre-paid consultation fee is fully refundable.
- Cancellations within 24 hours of the appointment are non-refundable, except in documented medical emergencies.
- Refunds for overpayments or billing errors are processed within 7 to 10 business days to the original payment method.

Common Billing Questions:
Q: Why was I charged more than my usual consultation fee?
A: Additional charges usually reflect diagnostic tests, procedures, or specialist add-ons performed during the visit that are billed separately from the base consultation fee.

Q: Can I get an itemized bill?
A: Yes, an itemized bill can be requested at the front desk or by phone, and is typically provided within 24 hours.

Q: What happens if my insurance claim is rejected?
A: The patient is responsible for the outstanding balance. The clinic can assist in resubmitting claims with corrected information if the rejection was due to a documentation error.

Outstanding Balance Inquiries:
Patients can check their outstanding balance by providing their patient ID or the phone number on file.
