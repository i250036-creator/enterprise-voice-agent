HORIZON HEALTH CLINIC — INSURANCE POLICY

Accepted Insurance Providers:
- SafeGuard Health Insurance
- National Family Care
- MediTrust Assurance
- WellPath Insurance Group
- BlueCircle Health Plans

Coverage Basics:
- General consultations are fully covered under all accepted plans, subject to a standard co-pay of 500 to 1000 (local currency) depending on plan tier.
- Specialist consultations (Cardiology, Orthopedics, Dermatology) require pre-authorization from the insurance provider for full coverage. Without pre-authorization, patients may be billed directly and can claim reimbursement separately.
- Emergency Department visits are covered under all plans without pre-authorization, regardless of provider.
- Dental procedures beyond basic checkups (fillings, extractions, root canals) are covered only under Premium tier plans. Basic checkups are covered under all tiers.
- Diagnostic tests (X-ray, blood work, ECG) are covered when ordered by a clinic doctor as part of a consultation.

Uninsured Patients:
Patients without insurance can pay out of pocket. The clinic offers a self-pay discount of 10 percent when payment is made in full at the time of the visit.

How to Verify Coverage:
Patients should bring their insurance card and a valid photo ID at every visit. The front desk verifies coverage before the consultation begins. For phone inquiries, the clinic can check coverage status if the patient provides their policy number.
