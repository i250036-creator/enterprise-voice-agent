HORIZON HEALTH CLINIC — GENERAL INFORMATION

Clinic Operating Hours:
Monday to Saturday: 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM
Sunday: 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM (Emergency Department only remains open 24/7)

Holiday Schedule:
The clinic operates on reduced hours (10:00 AM to 2:00 PM) on public holidays, except for the Emergency Department, which remains open at all times without exception.

Patient Visiting Hours (for admitted/in-patient care):
Morning session: 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM
Evening session: 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM
Maximum of 2 visitors per patient at a time. Children under 10 are not permitted in in-patient wards unless visiting immediate family in stable condition.

Parking and Access:
Free parking is available for the first 2 hours. Wheelchair access is available at the main entrance and all floors via elevator.

Contact and Location:
The clinic is located at 45 Horizon Avenue, and can be reached by phone during all operating hours. For emergencies, patients should call the Emergency Department line directly, available 24/7.

Language Support:
Front desk and phone support are available in English and Urdu. Select doctors also offer consultations in Punjabi upon request.
